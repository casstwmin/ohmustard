ABOUT US

Handmade with love

Oh mustard is a home business that sells customise homemade bags that are made with thick yarn. The bags are 100% handmade and we offer some customise option, such as choosing the colour, size, the style of the chain. There is also a few collections, the ice-ice baby Marshmallow series, Suede yarn collection, and the Twill collection. We also recommend others to properly check the measurements for the different sizes of the bag to make sure it is what they want. As well as the precautions about to how to handle and keep the bag in the best condition for a longer use.
